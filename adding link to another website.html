infopark

Infopark, Kochi is an information technology park situated in the city of Kochi, Kerala, India. Established in 2004 by the Government of Kerala, the park is spread over 260 acres of campus across two phases, housing 541 companies which employ more than 62,500 professionals as of 2022. The Phase 1 campus is fully developed spread over a 101 acres with more than 7 million sq ft of operational built-up space. Infopark Phase II is spread over 160 acres of campus area which would have a total built-up space of 8 million sq ft upon completion and is expected to employ more than 100,000 professionals
